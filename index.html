Welcome to the Example Site

Welcome to our example site! We are delighted to have you here. This site is designed to demonstrate the use of our accessibility library, yAccessibility.

Our goal is to make the internet a more accessible place for everyone. And also make it easier for websites owners to have their site a bit more accessible and comfortable without paying for widgets.

Here are some things you can do on this site:

  • Try out our accessibility tools
  • Change the contrast
  • Highlight links
  • Change the text size
  • And a lot more

Please feel free to explore the site and use the accessibility toolbar located in the bottom left corner. This is a link for example
